== Description ==
Convert More Visitors Into Customers, Subscribers and Social Fans
When your visitors land on your store, the biggest challenge is to keep their interest and convert them from simple visitors into customers, subscribers and social followers. OptinMate lets you create beautiful and and engaging popups, slide-ins and sticky bars that greatly increase your store's conversions and perfectly fit your store's theme and aesthetics.

Recover Abandoning Visitors Before They Leave Your Store
OptinMate tracks the visitor's behavior on your website and allows you to prompt them with a message right before they leave your store. This is a second chance to convert your visitors into email subscribers or paying customers with a coupon or a special offer, allowing you to get the most out of your marketing efforts

Multiple Smart Trigger Events
Timing is crucial when showing a popup. It can either improve or ruin the user experience. It is therefore very important to carefully choose the time your popup is going to be displayed. To ensure a great experience for your visitors, OptinMate gives you full control over when to show your popup, slide-in or sticky bar.

== Installation ==
1. From the dashboard of your site, navigate to Plugins --> Add New.
2. Select the Upload option and hit "Choose File."
3. When the popup appears select the optinmate.zip. file from your desktop.
4. Follow the on-screen instructions and wait as the upload completes.
5. When it's finished, activate the plugin via the prompt. A message will show confirming activation was successful.
6. After activating plugin navigate to optinmate option under wordpress left side menu.
7. click on "optinmate" > Choose your plan accordingly your needs.  

That's it! Just configure your settings as you see fit, and you're on your way to optinmate events in style.
